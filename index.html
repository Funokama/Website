About

After studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at ETH Zurich, I continued my academic path at the University of Bern, where I completed my Bachelor of Science in Economics and Computer Science in 2026. Since then, I have been deepening my knowledge of modern microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, and quantitative methods in the master's programme.

Alongside my studies, I work as an IT teaching assistant at the Institute of Marketing and Management (IMU) at the University of Bern, and as an IT consultant at Corporate Software, where I help organisations adopt Microsoft 365 solutions.
